## About

This project uses the **Critical Reasoning Tutor** skill to systematically analyze editorials, policy arguments, and opinion pieces. Each article is broken down into conclusion, premises, assumptions (Good/True/Happen), gap analysis, weakening strategies, vulnerable assumptions, and failure modes.

My own analyses are evaluated by the tutor with detailed feedback and scoring.
