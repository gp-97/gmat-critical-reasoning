## Summary

The UP government's One District, One Cuisine list of 208 dishes excludes all meat-based dishes — omitting iconic preparations like Tunday's Galawati, Awadhi biryani, and Kakori kabab. The author argues this sanitised curation erases the syncretic, cross-community nature of UP's culinary tradition and reflects a narrowing of what the state is willing to officially recognise as heritage.
